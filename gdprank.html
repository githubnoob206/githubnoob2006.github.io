HOW TO PLAY

  • 🎲Each round you're shown a random US state with its flag, name, and GDP (shown after placement).
  • 📦Tap one of the 10 slots to place it. Slot 1 holds the highest GDP state, Slot 10 the lowest.
  • 🔒Once placed, it's locked — no moving pieces around! Think carefully before tapping.
  • ⚡After all 10 states are placed, your arrangement is scored against the true GDP ranking.
  • 🏆Score is based on how close each state is to its correct rank. Exact = 1pt, one off = 0.6, two off = 0.3. Max 10!
  • 💡Tip: CA, TX, NY, FL dominate GDP. WY, VT, MT, AK tend to be smallest — but mid-tier states are tricky!
