HOW TO PLAY

  • 🎲Each round you're shown a random US state with its flag, name, and population.
  • 📦Tap one of the 10 slots to place it. Slot 1 holds the most populated group, Slot 10 the least.
  • 🔒Once placed, it's locked — no moving pieces around! Think carefully before tapping.
  • ⚡After all 10 states are placed, your arrangement is scored against the true population ranking.
  • 🏆Score is based on how close each state is to its correct rank. Exact = 100pts, one off = 60pts, two off = 30pts. Max 1000!
  • 💡Tip: CA, TX, FL, NY are always the giants. WY, VT, AK, ND are usually the tiny ones — but watch out for surprises in the middle!
